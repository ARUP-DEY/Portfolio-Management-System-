Techno India Salt Lake, also known as TISL, was established in 2001. Affiliated to Maulana Azad University of Technology, West Bengal, the institute is now known as Techno Main Salt Lake as it is the flagship college and the main placement centre for the Techno India Group of Colleges in West Bengal. TISL Kolkata offers 28 courses through four departments, namely Engineering, Basic Science & Humanities, Computer Application and Business School. The college has a wide range infrastructural facilities available to the students. These include SAP Labs, VR Lab, other laboratories, smart classes, library and hostels.

Techno Main Salt Lake FAQs

Techno Main Salt Lake FAQs

Q. What is the admission process of Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. Candidate can apply online/offline by registering in the website with valid score for each course. Admission in BTech is done through centralised counselling of WBJEE.

Q. When admission is open in Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. As the colleges accept various test scores for admission. So the admission dates vary for different courses but generally the admission open in February for Engineering courses.

Q. How good are faculties at Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. Faculties in Techno Main are from IIT, NIT and other prestigious colleges. Faculty quality is good in this college.

Q. How is the infrastructure in Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. The campus is spread in an area of 10 acre with all basic facilities and amenities.

Q. How can one get admission into BTech Lateral entry?

A. One can appear in the JELET exam and should have a diploma or B.sc degree.

Q. Does TISL give any scholarship?

A. Yes, TISL gives scholarship and tuition fee waiver on the basis of merit and need to deserving student. Also TISL has tie up with banks for loan assistance.

Q. What are the hostel facilities available in Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. Techno Main has limited hostel facilities but all sorts of PG are available near the campus.

Q. How are the placement at Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. The placement scenario is very good for branches like CSE, IT and ECE. These branches witness almost 80-90% placement every year and companies like, TCS, Microsoft, Intel regularly visit the campus.

Q. How good is Techno Main Salt Lake for BTech?

A. Techno Main Salt Lake is a good college for engineering .It could be considered under top 5 engineering college in Kolkata. It comes under top 10 Engineering colleges of WBJEE.

Q. What courses are offered by Techno Main Salt Lake?

A. Techno Main offers BTech, BTech (Lateral Entry), BBA, BHM, BCA, MCA, MTech and MBA.

Q. What are the specialisations offered in BTech?

A. Techno Main Salt Lake offers BTech in Computer Science, Civil, Electrical, Electronics and Communication, Applied Electronics and Instrumentation, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering and Food Technology.

Q. How good is Techno Main Salt Lake in terms of value for money?

A. As compared to ROI, the college gives decent opportunities and is visited by reputed companies like TCS with decent package. So in terms of value for money it can be put in the medium range.
